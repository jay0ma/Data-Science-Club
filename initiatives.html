Our Initiatives

We work on real-world business problems and build dashboards to provide actionable recommendations. Below are our active and planned initiatives with example dashboard mockups and the business questions we aim to answer.

Tokyo Restaurant — Real-world business experience

Create dashboards to monitor reservations, peak hours, menu performance, and customer segments. Advise on pricing and staffing.

  • Dashboards: Daily covers, table turnover, menu item performance
  • Business decisions: Pricing recommendations, staffing optimization
  • Customer segmentation: Frequent vs. occasional diners, promotion targeting

Hotel in Spain — Hospitality analytics

Analyze booking channels, occupancy, ADR (average daily rate), and guest reviews. Provide segmentation by guest type and seasonality.

  • Dashboards: Occupancy trends, revenue per available room (RevPAR)
  • Business decisions: Channel mix, promotional timing
  • Segmentation: Business vs leisure, repeat guests

Travel Magazine — Content & engagement

Build dashboards to track readership trends, content performance, ad revenue, and audience demographics for targeted recommendations.

  • Dashboards: Pageviews, session duration, conversion funnels
  • Business decisions: Editorial focus, ad pricing
  • Segmentation: Reader personas, geography

NGOs — Social impact analytics

Support NGOs with donor analytics, program monitoring dashboards, and impact measurement to improve outreach and funding allocation.

  • Dashboards: Donations, campaign performance, beneficiary metrics
  • Business decisions: Resource allocation, campaign targeting
  • Segmentation: Donor cohorts, high-value supporters

Small Japanese Businesses — Next phase

Shift focus to local small businesses in Japan: help with customer segmentation, simple dashboards, and practical advice to boost revenue and retention.

  • Dashboards: Customer visits, promotions ROI, inventory insights
  • Business decisions: Localized promotions, stock planning
  • Segmentation: Local customers, tourists, repeat visitors
